French Fries Reciepe

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Cold Water
  • Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Red Chilli Powder

Cooking Method

  1. Wash and peel the potatoes
  2. Cut them into thick slices like fingers
  3. Then mix the masalas together
  4. After the oil is heated cook the potatoes for 5 min
  5. Serve hot
